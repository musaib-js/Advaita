ADVAITA 2K23
Advaita, the annual techno-cultural fest of IIIT Bhubaneswar, is organized in the first week of March spanning over four days and four nights. Commenced with a motto of providing technocrats all over the country, a platform to showcase their technical skills and talent, Advaita has come a long way over the years since its inception.It is one of the eminent fests in the eastern region of the Indian subcontinent today which has made an impact on several college-going students across the country. With different, captivating themes each year, the fest has recorded immense participation in the past and this year will be no different. This Fest conducts a multitude of events encompassing 12 genres and more than 50 events.Get a chance to test your wits and prove your intelligence by enjoying the extraordinary opportunity of being a part of this astounding techno-cultural fest.
